About Hon. Elphas Shilosio

Hon. Elphas Shilosio is the elected Member of County Assembly (MCA) representing Murhanda Ward. He is committed to empowering communities, improving infrastructure, promoting education, and ensuring inclusive development.

His leadership focuses on accountability, service delivery, and sustainable growth for all residents of Murhanda Ward.
